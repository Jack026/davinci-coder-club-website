Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I join Da-Vinci Coder Club?

You can join by filling out our membership form above or attending any of our events. We welcome students from all departments and skill levels. No prior coding experience is required!

What activities does the club organize?

We organize workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, tech talks, project showcases, and networking events. We also work on collaborative projects and participate in national competitions.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership to Da-Vinci Coder Club is completely free for all ADTU students. We believe in making technology education accessible to everyone.

Can I contribute to projects even if I'm a beginner?

Absolutely! We have projects suitable for all skill levels. Our senior members mentor beginners, and we have specific beginner-friendly projects to help you learn and grow.

How often are meetings held?

We have regular weekly meetings every Friday at 4:00 PM, plus special events and workshops throughout the month. Check our events page for the latest schedule.

Can external organizations collaborate with us?

Yes! We welcome collaborations with companies, other universities, and tech organizations. Please contact us using the collaboration option above to discuss partnership opportunities.
