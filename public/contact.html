Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

How quickly will Jack026 respond to my message?

Jack026 typically responds within 2 minutes during office hours (9 AM - 6 PM, Mon-Fri). For urgent matters, we're available 24/7 and guarantee a response within 15 minutes. Emergency situations get immediate attention!

How can I join Da-Vinci Coder Club?

You can join by filling out our membership form above, selecting "Join Our Team" as the subject. Jack026 will personally review your application and guide you through the process. We welcome students from all departments and skill levels!

What's the best way to reach Jack026 for urgent matters?

For urgent matters, call +91 87654 32109 or send a WhatsApp message to +91 98765 43210. You can also mark your message as "urgent" in the contact form above. Emergency contact is available 24/7.

Can I get technical support for my projects?

Absolutely! Our team provides technical support for coding projects, debugging help, and mentorship. Jack026 and our senior members are always ready to help. Just select "Technical Support" in the contact form or join our Discord for real-time help.

How can I collaborate on events or partnerships?

We love collaborations! Use the "Partnership & Collaboration" option in our contact form, or email partnerships@davincicoders.adtu.ac.in directly. Jack026 handles all partnership discussions and will respond with detailed information about collaboration opportunities.

Was this helpful?

Do you have office hours for in-person visits?

Yes! Our office hours are Monday-Friday 9 AM to 6 PM, and Saturday 10 AM to 4 PM. Jack026 is usually available during these hours in Room CS-201. For weekend visits, please call ahead to confirm availability.
