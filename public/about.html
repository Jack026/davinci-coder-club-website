Our Mission

To cultivate a vibrant community of student innovators who blend creativity with technology, fostering the next generation of digital artisans and problem solvers.

Our Vision

To be the premier student-led technology club that bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical innovation, inspiring students to create meaningful digital solutions.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to technological excellence

2020

Foundation

Da-Vinci Coder Club was founded by a group of passionate computer science students with a vision to create a collaborative learning environment.

2021

First Hackathon

Organized our first internal hackathon with 50 participants, marking the beginning of our event-driven culture.

2022

National Recognition

Won multiple national-level hackathons and competitions, putting ADTU on the tech map.

2023

Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with leading tech companies for internships and placement opportunities.

2024

Present Day

Now 150+ active members strong, continuing to innovate and inspire the next generation of coders.
